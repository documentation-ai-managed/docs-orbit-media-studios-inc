## Quick Start

To begin managing your project docs:

1. Sign up at `https://orbitmedia.com/signup`
2. Log in and access your dashboard
3. Click "Create Project" to start a new doc space

## Adding Your First Page

1. Within your project, click "Add Page"
2. Enter your content and save

All your project documentation is now organized in one place!
