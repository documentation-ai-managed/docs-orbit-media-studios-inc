---

## Steps to Create a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Go to `https://orbitmedia.com/dashboard` and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Create Project'">
    Find the "Create Project" button and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Name and Save">
    Give your project a descriptive name and click Save. Your new documentation space is ready to use!
  </Step>
</Steps>
